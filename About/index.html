About us

COPPSARY was establish in 23th, October 2024 by 11 of Cambodia youth. Our main object on creating COPPSARY is to help farmer facilitate finding customers and the Cambodia marketing, Specially we aim to bring investment in agriculture. On the other hand, We will bring our farmers output to the international stage and threat into local and global marketing. Moreover, We will bring the new discovery and technique that evolve agriculture sector, and drive the population to understand about digital due to the policy of the government.


Due to these factors, We create COPPSARY to create the connection between farmers and entrepreneur, so They can trade their product in a global scale.


In this operation we fully hope that farm and entrepreneur will experiences this with convenient related to agriculture.
